About Us

Welcome to the Pittsburgh Quantum Repository (PQR), a database of molecular quantum properties. Search for molecules by name or browse by categories to obtain a 3D view, thermodynamic and physical properties. Conveniently link to molecular visualizations from lectures and lab courses via QR codes.
